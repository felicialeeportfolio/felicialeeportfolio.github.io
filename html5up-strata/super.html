Super&mama (2018)

When one mentions Singapore, what comes to your mind?

In collaboration with Supermama — a design and crafts gallery shop based in our sunny island.

As a self-proclaimed patriot, I enjoyed exploring iconic places and neighbourhoods to draw inspiration from locally-found imagery to come up with a series of Singapore souvenirs.


From our beloved grandmother’s handkerchief to glorifying the idea of traditional kopi making, every product tells a different story.


Skills: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, paper prototyping, material exploration
Timeline: 11 weeks, Designed with Heather Ong


Our group embodied the insights into porcelain wares, calendars, wax and textile using different mediums such as illustrator and photoshop. Here, you can find some of our favorite picks.
