Personal Branding (2018)

A glimpse into my personality

For this project, I wanted to reflect my personality through a series of awkward poses expressed using my graphical style. The different poses are created for people to find joy and amusement in as they relate to the characters.


The graphics tell a story about the girl who enjoys going for adventures but always find herself stuck in awkward circumstances.


Skills: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, paper prototyping, material exploration
Timeline: 13 weeks, Personal project


This series was designed and intended to be used as telegram stickers, water bottles, stationery and tea bag holder.
