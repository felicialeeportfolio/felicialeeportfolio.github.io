Competitions

2018 ST Aerospace Aircraft Seat (STAAS) Design Competition - First Place

This competition was conducted in collaboration between NUS and ST Engineering to envision design concepts that revolutionize the aircraft economy class for the next generation of aircraft seats.

Through a series of surveys and prototyping, our group came up with the 'Airmock' - a hammock seat design with sliding mechanisms at the top and bottom of the chair, completed with mesh backing that is both flexible and light, yet strong.

Timeline: 1 month, Designed with Janna Tan and Deborah Loh
